My Current Routine

I have been using EVP-3D Stim-Free Pre-Workout for a couple months now and have not felt the need to find something new. Some flavors have Creainte Anhydrous, while others have Monohydrate. Before and after training, I take XTEND BCAAs (use code "SOPHIA" for 20% off) mixed with NowSports L-Glutamine post-workout. Since I have intolerances to both dairy and gluten, I intake digestion enzymes with every meal. I am currently using Revive Digest Aid, but I have used Alani Nu Digestion Capsules in the past. As for probiotics, my favorite so far is from Just Thrive. I would like to mention that I've used Renew Life's Probiotics and did not feel they did anything for me. I tend to take greens 2-3 times a week in a smoothie. I have not found an exact brand I prefer, but I will update you all when I do.

I am always open to suggestions!
