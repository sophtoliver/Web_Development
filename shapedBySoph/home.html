About Me.

My fitness journey started in high school while I was working two jobs and going to school full time. Overwhelmed with post-graduation plans and the challenges of being a growing young adult, I used fitness as an outlet for relieving stress. Not having a gym partner or friends who took fitness seriously, I yearned for motivation to encourage myself to keep trying. I created this platform to bring together a community of supportive, like-minded individuals so that woman getting into the gym have a way to escape that feeling I felt so many years ago. Fitness comes in all shapes and sizes and can be illustrated in any and all places.

My goal is to inspire everyone to get in that mentality that YOU CAN DO IT. I am here to provide all the resources you need to become the best version of yourself.
